Thoughts On My All-Time Favorite Films

Blue Velvet

Directed By David Lynch

"Blue Velvet" is an incredibly powerful film. The acting, setting, score, and imagery all provide a one of a kind viewing experience that not many other films can match. In "Blue Velvet" Lynch follows Jeffrey Beaumont as he explores his home neighborhood upon returning from college. It is in this time that Jeffrey discovers the dark criminal underworld existing in his town.
