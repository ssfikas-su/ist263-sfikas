Film Reviews

Thoughts On Films I Have Seen Recently

Joker

Directed by Todd Phillips

Few movies have the impact that "Joker" has had in Hollywood. The film has dominated conversation in most film communities for the past few months after receiving the Golden Lion award at Venice Film Festival. The first comic book film to do so. The film has also been discussed and marketed as a more "serious" alternative to tradtional comic book films. Putting the controversy aside, "Joker" holds its own as a standalone character study of one of the most famous characters in both cinema and comic books...

Rushmore

Directed by Wes Anderson

Rushmore is the first live action Wes Anderson movie I have seen. It seems clear to me now why Wes Anderson is lauded as one of the most idiosyncratic directors of this generation. Rushmore is an incredibly charming and funny coming-of-age story with a unique visual style that Anderson would eventually become famous for. Jason Schwartzman and Bill Murray deliver great performances and have great comedic chemistry...
